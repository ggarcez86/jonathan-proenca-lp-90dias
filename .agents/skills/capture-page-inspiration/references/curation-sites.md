# Curation Sites — Search Targets

Use these as primary hunting grounds for premium capture page references. Each entry includes the base URL and a suggested search pattern. When `web_fetch` on the gallery itself is blocked, use `web_search` with `site:` operators.

## Tier 1 — Design-First Galleries (highest signal)

### Land-book
- URL: https://land-book.com
- Why: Curated premium landing pages, filterable by style and industry
- Search: `site:land-book.com mentorship`, `site:land-book.com coaching`, `site:land-book.com workshop`
- Filter by: "Business", "Education", "Course" categories

### Lapa Ninja
- URL: https://www.lapa.ninja
- Why: Strong on conversion-focused pages, lots of infoproduct and course LPs
- Search: `site:lapa.ninja landing page course`, `site:lapa.ninja webinar`
- Categories to browse: "Courses", "Events", "Consulting"

### One Page Love
- URL: https://onepagelove.com
- Why: Single-purpose pages — almost always the right format for capture
- Search: `site:onepagelove.com event`, `site:onepagelove.com webinar`
- Tags to filter: "Event", "Conference", "Landing Page"

### SaaS Landing Page
- URL: https://saaslandingpage.com
- Why: SaaS tier often overlaps with premium AI-forward aesthetic
- Use for: hero treatments, gradient patterns, modern form styling

## Tier 2 — Aesthetic Galleries

### Godly
- URL: https://godly.website
- Why: High-end web design curation, strong on typography and motion
- Use for: moodboard inspiration, not direct capture page structure

### Minimal Gallery
- URL: https://minimal.gallery
- Why: Elegant, restrained aesthetic — matches "sofisticado" positioning

### Httpster
- URL: https://httpster.net
- Why: Broad modern web design curation

### SiteInspire
- URL: https://www.siteinspire.com
- Why: Filterable by style (Minimal, Bold, Typographic) and subject (Education, Business)

## Tier 3 — Specific Niche Hunts

### Product Hunt launches of mentorship/coaching products
- URL: https://www.producthunt.com
- Search: `"mentorship" site:producthunt.com`, `"career coaching" site:producthunt.com`
- Follow launch pages back to their marketing sites

### Dribbble / Behance (use cautiously)
- Concept designs, not live pages — useful only for aesthetic patterns
- Search: `executive mentorship landing page site:dribbble.com`

### Brazilian infoproducer ecosystem
- Search open Google for: `mentoria executiva página de captura`, `workshop alta performance inscrição`, `treinamento carreira C-level landing page`
- Known premium players in BR to check by name: surface via search rather than assume

## Open Google Patterns

Use these templates, swapping the niche term:

```
"{niche}" landing page capture form premium inspiration
"{niche}" workshop registration page high end
"{niche}" webinar signup landing page 2026
high ticket "{niche}" opt-in page
"{niche}" mentorship waitlist page
```

Good niche terms to try for this project:
- executive coaching
- career acceleration
- leadership mentorship
- AI career transformation
- C-suite coaching
- promotion methodology

## Red Flags — Skip These

- Pages where the primary CTA is "Buy Now" or "Get Started" leading to checkout → sales page, not capture
- Pages with pricing tables → sales page
- Pages built on visible low-end templates (Wix blocks, ClickFunnels stock, Kajabi defaults)
- Pages with more than 3 CTAs competing for attention → not a focused capture page
- Blog posts with an inline signup → not a dedicated capture page
